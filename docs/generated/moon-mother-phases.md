# Moon-Mother 16-Phase Names — Minimal Abbreviations

An alternate 16-division naming scheme for the synodic cycle, abbreviations of each name. Every name ends in "Moon", which carries no
information once all 16 share it, so it is dropped; the abbreviation is the shortest
prefix of the *leading* word(s) that stays unambiguous across the whole set.

Index 1 is the **new moon** (Dark Moon), matching moonphase's `0° = new` convention
so this scheme lines up with `--divisions 16` indexing.

| # | Name | Shortest unique form |
|---|------|----------------------|
| 1 | Dark Moon | `Da` |
| 2 | Sickle Moon | `Si` |
| 3 | Crescent Moon | `Cr` |
| 4 | Emerging Moon | `Em` |
| 5 | First Quarter Moon | `1Q` |
| 6 | Swelling Moon | `Sw` |
| 7 | Gibbous Moon | `Gb` |
| 8 | Culminating Moon | `Cu` |
| 9 | Full Moon | `Fl` |
| 10 | First Waning Moon | `1W` |
| 11 | Disseminating Moon | `Di` |
| 12 | Transporting Moon | `Tr` |
| 13 | Last Quarter Moon | `LQ` |
| 14 | Yielding Moon | `Yd` |
| 15 | Balsamic Moon | `Bl` |
| 16 | Immanent Moon | `Im` |

These names and codes ship as [`examples/moon-mother-16.csv`](../../examples/moon-mother-16.csv) — use them with
`moonphase --divisions 16 --format heatmap --tint index --labels @examples/moon-mother-16.csv`.
